About Us

Slice N Grind, established on December 9, 2023, is a cafe/ restaurant located on Poblacion, Santa Maria, Bulacan, between Kodak Sta. Maria and Mercury Drugstore. Specializing in moist and chiffon cakes, we cater custom orders for weddings, birthdays, and other celebrations. Aside from cakes, we also serve variety of food such as pastries, rice meals, pasta, snacks and drinks.
